### **Somerset Recreation Club** (SRC)

<img class="content-image" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/images/somerset-src.jpg" alt="Somerset Recreation Club pool and grounds" />

**Somerset Recreation Club** (SRC) is a family-oriented community club with swimming pool, kiddie pool, and tennis/pickleball court facilities. We offer Summer memberships (May – Labor Day), swim team and lessons, tennis team and lessons, pickleball lessons and adult drop-in play, and weekly Adult lap swim. We also offer Off-season memberships (September – May) for court use and weekly Adult lap swim. Our facility is located in Bellevue, Washington and we have some of the most spectacular views of Lake Washington and Seattle.

### Mary Wayte Pool

**Mary Wayte Pool** is a well-maintained indoor swimming facility located in Mercer Island, Washington. Named after Olympic gold medalist Mary Wayte, the pool serves as a hub for the local community, offering a variety of programs including lap swimming, swimming lessons, and competitive training.

The facility features a standard 25-yard pool with multiple lanes, a warm and welcoming environment, and professional staff dedicated to supporting swimmers of all ages and skill levels. It is a popular venue for swim meets, fitness enthusiasts, and families seeking a reliable space for aquatic activities year-round.
