### Juni Wu — Head Coach

<div class="instructor-lead" markdown="1">
<img class="content-image content-image--portrait" src="{{ site.baseurl }}/images/juni-wu.jpg" alt="Juni Wu, Head Coach" />

With over 14 years of competitive swimming experience, Coach Juni won most of the championship titles in China in her age group in 50 m and 100 backstrokes before relocating to the U.S. with her family. Since 2016, she has been a dedicated coach member of USA Swimming, continuing her passion for the sport.
</div>

> I've learned that success is built on belief, passion, and perseverance. Staying positive, persistent, and embracing challenges with a smile are values I've carried throughout my swimming career, and I strive to instill these in every swimmer I coach.
